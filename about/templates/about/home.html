Who is Unky Rick?

During the summer holidays after the school year, your parents send you to stay with a distant relative you met a long time ago when you were a kid. All you know about him is his nickname, "Unky Rick." He's an elderly man who lives alone. Despite being somewhat reclusive, you remember having fun together during previous encounters.

Unky Rick has travelled extensively, but his health has recently declined. This led him to move closer to your family, and he now needs some assistance.

Upon arriving at his address, you are shocked to find a large house and a still robust and humorous old man greeting you at the door. After a brief conversation, you realise just how clever and intriguing your Unky Rick truly is.

You feel a strong desire to ask Unky Rick many questions. To do so, you'll need to be prepared to learn new things, as your passion for discovering will surely be sparked.
